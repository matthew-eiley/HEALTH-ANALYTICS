How to Read This Chart

Each square represents a day. The color intensity shows how well you met your sleep hour goals:

  • Dark green: Excellent (92.5%+ of needed hours)
  • Medium green: Good (85-92.5% of needed hours)
  • Light green: Fair (77.5-85% of needed hours)
  • Very light green: Poor (70-77.5% of needed hours)
  • Gray: Very poor (<70% of needed hours) or no data
